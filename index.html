Papercut Demo

Requirements: A computer that supports WebGL, and a controller that supports the gamepad API. If you're on a phone, try this direct link instead.

Papercut is a 2.5 dimensional platforming RPG, where you navigate a 3D world as a 2D character. It runs on the Godot game engine. As you explore the world, you'll find new characters to recruit to your party, and new abilities to help you navigate the world.

Controls

Objective: Recruit the other characters to your party, and defeat the boss!

  • Move: Control stick (or drag on left half of screen)
  • Jump/Talk: A or B button (or touch right half of screen)
